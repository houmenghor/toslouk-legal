Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2026 | Last Updated: July 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tos Louk ("we," "our," "us"). We operate the e-commerce platform and service located at https://toslouk.me (the "Service"). We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring your personal information is protected with the highest standards of security.

This Privacy Policy outlines our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our website, particularly when using single sign-on (SSO) OAuth mechanisms provided by Google and Facebook.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal data to authenticate users, personalize account profiles, and facilitate transactions.

Google OAuth Data

When you sign in using Google, we request access to your basic public profile details. We collect and store your Full Name, Email Address, and Profile Picture URL (Avatar).

Facebook OAuth Data

When you sign in using Facebook, we request access to your Public Profile (Name, ID, Avatar URL) and Email Address. If your Facebook account lacks a public email address, we automatically generate a fallback registration email (e.g., id@facebook.toslouk.com) to maintain account security.

Transactional & Checkout Data

To fulfill e-commerce purchases, we collect delivery coordinates, including your Shipping Address, Billing Address, and Phone Number. We do not store your bank credentials or raw payment cards; payments are processed securely by external, certified payment networks (such as ABA Pay).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To verify, register, and manage your user account.
  • To personalize your display profile name and avatar image.
  • To process, ship, and deliver the items you buy from our online store.
  • To send order status notifications, invoices, and handle customer support tickets.
  • To secure our site, prevent fraud, and comply with transactional regulations in Cambodia.

4. Sharing Your Data

We value your trust. We do **not** sell, rent, trade, or distribute your personal data. We share your information only with essential service providers:

  • Couriers & Shipping Providers: We provide your name, shipping address, and phone number to local delivery services to deliver your packages.
  • Payment Gateways: Order totals and checkout details are shared securely with processors to confirm transactions.

5. Data Deletion Instructions & Rights

In compliance with Google API User Data Policies and Meta's developer regulations for Facebook Login, we provide absolute control to users over their personal data. You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request data deletion:

  • Account Settings (Recommended): Log in to the application, go to your User Dashboard, click Settings, and choose Delete Account. This permanently purges your account.
  • Support Email: Send an email request directly to our developer at support@toslouk.me. Please state your registered email address and name.
  • Facebook Settings Revocation: You can remove your linked account through your Facebook profile:
    Settings & Privacy > Settings > Apps and Websites > Select 'Tos Louk' > Click Remove.
  • Google App Revocation: You can remove our application's access via your Google account settings:
    Google Account > Security > Third-party apps with account access > Select 'Tos Louk' > Click Remove Access.

Once requested, all linked social account identifiers, full names, profile email addresses, shopping wishlists, and avatars will be permanently scrubbed from our database within 24 hours.
